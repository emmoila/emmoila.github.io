Dr Raphela Dental Surgery

Your first step towards healthier teeth and a better lifestyle.

At Dr Raphela Dental & Cosmetic Surgery, we are devoted to improving the appearance, health and self-confidence of our patients in a relaxed caring atmosphere. Combining state of the art facilities, equipment and techniques, you can trust your smile with us.

Care ranges from single tooth replacement to full mouth rehabilitation, using laser equipment, painless endodontics, periodontal procedures, teeth whitening, restylane, perlane, & Botox. Every detail in the practice is directed toward excellence in patient care by a friendly staff of career professionals.

We recognize that a lot of people have fears and apprehensions toward dental treatment and we make every effort to ease those fears by providing a positive and pain free experience.
We are located in Limpopo at 52 Marone Street, Burgersfort 1150.| @Dr Benny Choeu Medical Centre. The surgery was established in 2008 by Dr Raphela, who has been practicing since 2008.

Dr. Raphela holds a Bachelors in Dental Science (UL) and a post-graduate diploma in Aesthetic Dentistry from University of Western Cape.
